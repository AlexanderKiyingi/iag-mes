How scheduled services become work orders
STEP 1
Planner schedules
Pick asset, date/time, PM template, technician. Single screen.
STEP 2
Job created
Scheduled job (SJ-XXX) appears on the calendar. Frequency is captured.
STEP 3
Draft WO
A draft work order is auto-generated with template's checklist + parts.
STEP 4
Auto-activate
On the day, status flips Scheduled → Open. Tech sees it on Mobile PWA.
STEP 5
Execute & close
Tech executes checklist, logs findings. Next instance auto-scheduled.
What's automated:
  • WO numbering, asset, type, priority, estimated duration, parts list, checklist, assignee
  • Status transitions (Scheduled → Open on due date · Open → Overdue if not started by end of shift)
  • For recurring jobs (weekly/monthly/quarterly), the next occurrence is automatically created when one closes
  • Parts reservations: stock is reserved when a draft WO is created, freed if the job is cancelled
  • Notifications: assignee is notified 24h before, supervisor is notified if overdue
